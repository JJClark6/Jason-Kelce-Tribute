Jason Kelce

One of the best eagles of all time. With it possibly being his last year in the NFL I would like to make a tribute to his Hall of Fame Career.

About The Eagles' Legend

Jason Kelce was born on November 5th, 1987, in Westlake, Ohio. He attended Cleveland Heights High School where he played both linebacker and running back. He later switched to offensive line after redshirting his initial year at Cincinnati

Jason was taken in the 6th round of the 2011 draft by the Philidelphia Eagles. He signed a four year rookie contract and became the starting center. Kelce had an outsnading 2013 season where he started all 16 games and helped the eagles make the playoffs.

From here Kelce kept improving and eventually came one of the best centers in the league year in and year out.

Jason Kelce seems to have an endless list of accolades: 7 time Pro Bowler, 5 time First Team All Pro, Superbowl Champion. And most of all he captured the hearts of Philly fans all over the world.
